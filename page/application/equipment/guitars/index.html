Just a list of my favorite guitars. I am convinced, a guitar only can be good, if it is played regularly. Of course, you cannot make a broomstick a good guitar, so the prerequisites should match.

Mind the strings, as they are very, very important. I personally only play Thomastik strings, so Thomastik-Infeld on the guitars mentioned below. More, if a guitar has no vibrato, I prefer the size .011 to .052, otherwise normal .010 to .046.

  • PRS Hollowbody II Piezo Feels like a part of me. Does, whatever I want. Maybe I am a bit too young for this guitar.
  • PRS McCarty 594 Joe Walsh Incredible piece of a guitar. Must be played every day and does not stop becoming better then, whereby I never know what exactly became better. The guitar or me? Reminds me of a once very beloved woman.
  • PRS 509 Wood library. Swamp ash body and maple neck.
  • PRS Custom 24 Piezo If you are in doubt, take your Custom 24.
  • Musicman Cutlass SSS Often preferred over my PRS 509. It's a real cutlass, man. Forget about a Strat.

So I am a PRS-Player? Ok, talking about the two guitars first listed above: they are simply incredible.

My Cutlass is incredible as well, but in another respect. This guitar once convinced me to keep handling with cheap Strat sounds.
