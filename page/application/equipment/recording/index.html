Hardware

  • Focusrite Scarlet 18i20 Runs brilliantly on Linux systems, even better than on Windows, since Linus himself should have written the driver. Can be configured like hell using the GUI from Geoffrey Bennet. See alsa-scalett-gui on github.com. One of those arguments to never use Windows again.
  • Neumann KH 120 II Not worth mentioning. After calibrating them (you need a matching mic and the software), they sound fantastic.
  • Headphone amp and plenty of headphones Nothing to say further here, except there are some headphones of remarkable quality in my box.
  • Cable Vovox. If I am running out with them - come on, you should know.
