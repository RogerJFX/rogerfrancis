Little Wing

Main version
Date: Year 2000
Using a basic Fender Champ with setup described below.
Other version
Date: Year 2000
Same guitar, Synergy preamp with Tweed module, stereo chorus and things. Pretty much sound shaping.

People say, nobody could really play this one like the great master. Agreed. This one isn't that easy, since it is not only technically a bit harder to play. But who says, it must sound like Jimi's version on the other hand? I like it like so. That's why I play it this way. If you don't like it, I'm absolutely ok with it.

Mind the simple setup on first version. It wasn't meant to become a song here. This indeed would have been impossible. So this take should remain a sample, just as a snapshot of someone's space of time in life. The other version's setup isn't described here.

Instruments

  • Guitar: PRS McCarty (Thomastic Infeld 11-52 strings).
  • Amp: Fender Champ from 1978 with TAD tubes.
  • First microphone: Sennheiser E-906.
  • Second microphone: Shure SM 57.
  • Recording device: Tascam DR-40.
  • DAW: Harrison Mixbus 10 Pro.
