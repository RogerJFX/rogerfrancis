Instruments

  • Theme guitar: PRS Custom 24 Piezo.
  • Solo guitar: PRS McCarty (Thomastic Infeld 11-52 strings).
  • Bass guitar: Ibanez fretless 5 string with an additional piezo pickup (Thomastic flatwound strings) over an En-Voice Mindprint.
  • Backing vocal's mic: Sennheiser MD 431 II
  • High bells: MIDI with Proteus 2000 expander.
  • Drums: MIDI. Black Pearl Drumset sounds provided by the DAW.
  • DAW: Harrison Mixbus 10 Pro.
