Foxy Annette

Ok, it's just another love song, but why not playing it this way?

Instruments

  • Guitar: Charvel in Tele style, shaped and with tremolo and Fishman PUs.
  • Amp: Synergy SYN-2, Plexi module.
  • Bass guitar: Ibanez fretless 5 string with an additional piezo pickup (Thomastic flatwound strings) over an En-Voice Mindprint.
  • DAW: Harrison Mixbus 10 Pro.
